Heat & mobility playbook

  • Midday is A/C time. Early September runs ~90°F with 75–79% humidity; it eases late-month. Do outdoor stops before ~11 AM or after ~5 PM (evenings dip to a pleasant ~72°F) and stay indoors 11–3.
  • Let cars & streetcars do the walking. Take door-to-door rideshares, and hop a streetcar when the route is short and flat.
  • Take seating breaks. The WWII Museum and the Aquarium have seating everywhere — use it on hot days.
  • Pack: refillable water, a cooling towel, a personal misting fan, wide-brim hats, sunscreen, and one umbrella (sun and pop-up storms). Alternate every cocktail with water.
  • Reset points: a hotel-pool break and a snoball keep the whole crew (and the 7-year-old) from melting.
